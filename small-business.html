The Four Leaks

Every local business leaks money in the same four places

It doesn't matter whether you run a clinic, a gym, a law office, a repair shop, or a studio. The leaks are identical. Only the wording changes.

Leak 01 · The missed call

Nobody picked up

The leak: you were with a customer, it was after hours, or it was lunch. The phone rang out, and they called the next name on the list.

An agent answers every call and message the moment it lands, day or night. It captures who they are and what they need, and books them if it can.

Leak 02 · The slow lead

You replied the next morning

The leak: a form fill or DM at 8 PM that you saw at 9 AM. By then they'd already messaged three of your competitors.

The Speed to Lead agent replies in 60 seconds, asks the questions you'd ask, and puts qualified people straight on your calendar.

Leak 03 · The no show

Booked isn't the same as showed

The leak: the appointment was on the books, nobody confirmed it, and the slot sat empty. You paid for that lead twice.

Automatic confirmations and reminders go out at the right moments, and cancellations get backfilled from your waitlist instead of vanishing.

Leak 04 · The dead list

Your best customers forgot you exist

The leak: hundreds of past customers who liked your work and simply drifted. No reminder, no reason to come back.

The Reactivation agent runs campaigns to the list you already own. Revenue from people who already trust you, at nearly zero acquisition cost.
